# Roadmap and Maintenance Plan

JediKG is developed and maintained by the **LACAM@ARA Team3 Laboratory** at the Italian **University of Bari Aldo Moro**, within the **Department of Computer Science**.

The project is one of the principal maintained tools of our laboratory and reflects our ongoing research activity in knowledge graphs, semantic technologies, ontology-based methods, and AI-assisted data enrichment. JediKG is designed to support reproducible experimentation, flexible knowledge graph construction, and integration with downstream research workflows.

For more information about the laboratory, the research group, and related projects, see:

- **Laboratory website:** [LACAM@ARA Team3](http://ara.di.uniba.it/t3/)
- **Laboratory GitHub:** [GitHub Organization](https://github.com/ara-t3)

## Contributing to Our Project

Bugs, feature requests, and usage questions are managed through the project GitHub issue tracker. Issues are reviewed periodically by the maintainers and prioritized according to severity, reproducibility, and relevance to the project roadmap.

Dependencies are reviewed during each development cycle. Compatibility with external tools, such as reasoners, PyKEEN, and SPARQL-related libraries, is monitored, and dependency updates are incorporated when they improve stability, security, or interoperability.

Documentation is updated alongside new features and releases. Examples, configuration files, and API references are maintained to support reproducibility and onboarding of new users.
